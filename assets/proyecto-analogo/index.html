Project: Proyecto Análogo

Proyecto Análogo is an original science fiction game concept that explores parallel realities, mystery, and environmental storytelling through immersive first-person gameplay.

As the creator of the project, I developed the visual direction, gameplay interface, promotional assets, and world-building concepts to establish a consistent identity across the experience. The project combines graphic design, UI concepts, AI-assisted image generation, and game development prototypes to communicate the atmosphere and narrative of the fictional universe.

The result is a multidisciplinary creative project that showcases my ability to transform an original idea into a cohesive visual experience while integrating branding, concept design, and digital content creation.
