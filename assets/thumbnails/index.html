Project: Thumbnails

YouTube Thumbnail Design Thumbnails is a collection of YouTube cover designs created to capture attention, communicate content instantly, and increase visual impact across gaming and educational videos.

Each thumbnail was designed with a strong focus on composition, color contrast, typography, and storytelling. The workflow combines traditional graphic design principles with AI-assisted image generation and photo manipulation to create eye-catching visuals that remain clear and readable even at small sizes.

The project showcases my ability to design high-performing digital assets that balance creativity, branding, and marketing objectives while adapting to different audiences and content styles.
