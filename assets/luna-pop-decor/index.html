Project: Luna Pop Decor

Luna Pop Decor is an event decoration brand specializing in personalized celebrations, balloon arrangements, and custom party experiences.

The project focused on creating a vibrant and recognizable visual identity that reflects the brand's playful personality while maintaining a clean and professional appearance. Alongside the logo design, I developed a series of marketing materials—including flyers, printed banners, social media graphics, and realistic mockups—to ensure a consistent visual presence across both digital and printed media.

The final result is a cohesive branding system designed to increase brand recognition, attract new customers, and communicate the company's services in a visually engaging and memorable way.
