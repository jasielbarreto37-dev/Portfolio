Project: Voices

Voices is an English language learning project focused on helping students build confidence through practical communication and engaging educational experiences.

The visual identity was designed to feel modern, approachable, and energetic, reflecting the school's mission of making language learning accessible and enjoyable. Beyond the logo, the project included a complete set of branded materials, stationery, and promotional assets to ensure a consistent presence across both print and digital media.

The result is a clean and flexible brand identity that supports the school's growth while communicating professionalism, trust, and a welcoming learning environment.
